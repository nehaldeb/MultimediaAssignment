Basic Tools

Basic multimedia tools include simple programs that allow users to edit and enhance media such as images, sound, and video files.

Painting & Drawing Tools

These tools allow artists to create digital illustrations and paintings. Popular examples include Adobe Photoshop and Corel Painter.

OCR Software

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) software converts different types of documents, such as scanned paper documents, PDFs, or images captured by a camera, into editable and searchable data.

Sound Editing Programs

Sound editing programs, such as Audacity or Adobe Audition, allow users to manipulate audio files for a variety of purposes, from podcasts to film production.

Animation Devices & Digital Movies

These tools help in creating animations and digital movies. Examples include Blender for 3D animation and Adobe Animate for 2D animation.

Office Suites

Office suites, such as Microsoft Office and Google Workspace, include word processors, spreadsheets, and presentation tools to handle all office tasks efficiently.

Authoring Tools

There are different types of authoring tools such as card/page-based, icon-based, and time-based tools that help in creating multimedia content. Examples include Adobe Captivate and Articulate Storyline.
